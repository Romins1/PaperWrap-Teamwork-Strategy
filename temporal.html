3. Aligned Marketing & Sales Action Plans (HOW)

Select an active operational area below to display specific stages, responsibilities, and workflows.

Direct Omnichannel Outreach (Stage 1)

Roma maintains direct management over four LinkedIn Sales Navigator accounts, pushing out steady messaging pipelines. For key strategic targets where LinkedIn falls short, we deploy physical postal direct mail, targeted emails, and direct cold calling to build a reliable outbound base.

CRM Ingestion & Data Sanitation (Stage 2)

Our absolute priority is that each team member inputs their secured leads directly into HubSpot if time allows. If a closer is on the road or busy with demonstrations, they simply email the lead's details to Roma, who inputs them into the HubSpot CRM to ensure the central system stays up to date.

Stage 03: Closing Assignment & Team Ownership
Closers: Jurre (Int.) & Ángel (Iberia) Rule: Absolute Personal Ownership
Absolute Lanes

No team members modify or intervene in another closer's agenda or client communications. Closers maintain complete, independent responsibility over their assigned pipeline.

Roma & Lourdes (Sales Support)

Roma and Lourdes participate directly in the sales pipeline, attending to incoming leads from all channels and independently managing any custom leads they secure.

Dynamic Safety Valve

If Jurre's international pipeline overflows, the Spain closer unit (Ángel + Roma and Lourdes providing backup) temporarily helps handle international qualification calls.

Follow-up & Activity Control (Stage 4)

To ensure no lead is forgotten or left stale for more than two weeks, the team utilizes a strict, structured follow-up layout in HubSpot. We add notes directly to client cards, set tasks with due dates, and classify leads from highest potential (high interest) to lowest potential, guaranteeing that we maintain continuous communication and active focus on promising opportunities.

Co-Creative Ideation Loop (Stage 1)

Roma and Jurre partner closely to formulate high-impact social ideas. Jurre captures raw footage in the Waalwijk center or on-site during client visits, while Roma drafts corporate hooks highlighting real-world technical packaging advantages.

Structuring, Adaptation & Brand Filtering (Stage 2)

The team drops creative concepts, visual ideas, and text pitches in our single shared Excel workbook. Ilse and André audit these concepts to ensure complete alignment with PaperWrap’s tone of voice, values, and branding before moving them onto the live publishing calendar.

Asset Construction & Execution (Stage 3)

Roma leads overall execution, leveraging Canva and raw clips. Lourdes steps in as key operational support to construct vertical assets and post videos (primarily targeting TikTok), creating a smooth, passive, and low-stress visual showcase.

Pre-Campaign Assessment (Stage 1)

Roma conducts a deep keyword analysis targeting logistics and enwrapped terms. We identify exact high-intent queries (e.g. "pallet wrapper" or "industrial wrap machinery") where competitors are active to find high-converting search terms.

Budget Adequacy & Bidding Structure (Stage 2)

Setting clear, non-wasteful daily and monthly budgets (integrated directly into the overall marketing calculations). We prioritize bid strategies that target high search-intent leads while minimizing average Cost-Per-Click.

Copywriting, Ad Assets & Execution (Stage 3)

Drafting compelling ad variations and configuring direct call extensions. Roma sets up clean landing page destinations that directly address target concerns regarding cost, ROI, and sustainable wrapping.

Performance Control & Optimization Loop (Stage 4)

Periodic evaluation of keyword quality score, ad strength, and landing conversions. Roma monitors search term reports weekly to add irrelevant queries to the negative keywords list, preventing wasteful clicks.

Request Quotation & Spatial Budgeting (Stage 1)

Reviewing cost-estimates for Fira de Barcelona Hispack space allocations. We analyze required power, physical machinery footprint requirements, safety, and core services from the event registrar to lock down initial pricing parameters.

All-Inclusive Campaign Plan Design (Stage 2)

A comprehensive, unified blueprint covering all logistics: physical stand layout design, scheduled travel arrangements, staff roster coordinates, and specific event-related marketing collateral development (via Inkscape). We align this with our proactive Sales Navigator campaign to secure custom stand meetings with targeted leads before the show begins.
